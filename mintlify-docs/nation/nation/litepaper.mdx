# What is Nation?

Nation is an all-in-one platform designed to let anyone quickly create and manage AI-powered digital businesses. Just as leading web2 platforms simplified traditional e-commerce, Nation makes it easy to deploy fully customized, autonomous AI agent. Whether you’re building a personal assistant or a smart online storefronts — NO CODING IS REQUIRED.

We handle the complexities behind the scenes, allowing you to concentrate fully on innovation and growth.

# Why This Matters Now

With AI rapidly transforming how we live and work, there's an urgent need to simplify its use.

Agentic innovation evolved from simple Chats (2024) to Agentic Actions and Tools (2025) and is headed towards Fully Autonomous Agentic Teams (2026 and beyond).

However, building reliable agentic apps often involves steep learning curves, technical complexity, and disjointed monetization paths. This limits innovation and restricts participation to a small group of highly technical users.

Nation changes this dynamic by simplifying AI technology, making it accessible, customizable, and usable for everyone—allowing anyone to actively participate in and benefit from the growing AI economy.

# Platform Architecture

Nation is built on a robust, scalable architecture designed for smooth growth. The IntentKit framework forms the core, enabling modular skill creation.

Onchain smart contracts ensure transparency and reliability, while offchain components manage more intensive tasks. Integrated wallets, APIs, and secure hosting ensure seamless operations from agent creation through large-scale deployment.

# Built for Consumers, Entrepreneurs and Enterprises

Nation supports a diverse range of users, providing flexible solutions for every business scenario:

- **Individuals:** Quickly build personal assistants for customized automation and productivity, adopting a consumer-friendly, pay-per-transaction model that allows easy usage without upfront costs.
- **Entrepreneurs:** Create public storefronts that monetize unique AI services externally, participating in a revenue-sharing model where Nation and creators both benefit from the agent's growth and success.
- **Enterprises:** Access a fully white-label solution designed specifically for enterprise needs, utilizing licensing agreements to deploy secure, branded AI-powered platforms, integrating seamlessly into existing business infrastructures and operations.

These multiple revenue streams—consumer transactions, entrepreneurial revenue sharing, and enterprise licensing—ensure Nation’s sustainability and ongoing growth.
